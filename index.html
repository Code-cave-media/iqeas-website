Excellence in Marine & Offshore Engineering

IQEAS Offshore Engineering Pvt. Ltd. is a specialized engineering firm delivering high-quality accommodation and technical building solutions for the offshore industry. Our expertise lies in providing efficient, ergonomic, and compliant engineering services tailored to offshore living quarters, workspaces, and modular units.

With a team bringing over 20 years of combined industry experience, we have executed 300+ projects across platforms, FPSOs, barges, and rigs worldwide. From concept through completion, we prioritize safety, comfort, and compliance with class rules, SOLAS, ILO, and MLC standards.

Engineering Excellence at Sea and Shore

  • Specialized marine structural engineering and analysis
  • Comprehensive offshore consulting and project planning
  • Professional maintenance and support services for marine infrastructure with focus on safety and compliance standards

At IQEAS, we are committed to innovation, quality, and client-focused delivery. Our goal is to reduce weight and cost, enhance safety and habitability, and exceed expectations through each phase of offshore engineering.
